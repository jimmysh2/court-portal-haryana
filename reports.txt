 
Format of Report (Based on Rows) 
1. For given Range of Dates-District wise/Court wise data 
2. For given District/Court- Day/Month/year wise 
District Wise/Court Wise Data 
1. Give an option to choose dates 
a. Any Date Range (upto previous day)-  
b. Particular month (upto previous month)- of that year 
c. Particular Year (upto previous year) 
2. Give an option to choose District wise/Court wise 
a. If court wise chosen- give an option to choose District 
b. If District wise chosen- No further action required 
Date wise Data 
1. Give an option to choose District wise/Court wise 
a. If court wise chosen- give an option to choose District 
b. If District wise chosen- No further action required 
2. Choose Daywise/Monthwise/Year wise 
a. Day wise- For the current month, upto previous day 
b. Month wise-For the current year, upto previous month 
c. Year wise-upto previous year 
Alerts 
1. Pending data entry for yesterday 
2. Unfilled Data 
Submit Button Flag- Do not fetch the data in Report if final submit not done by naib court
